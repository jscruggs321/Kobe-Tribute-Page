The Life of Kobe Bryant

  • 1978 - Kobe Bean Bryant is born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania.
  • 1996 - Kobe wins National High School Player of the Year award, is drafted by the Hornets as the 13th selection in the 1st round, and is traded to the Los Angeles Lakers.
  • 1998 - Makes his 1st All-Star apperance as the youngest all-star ever.
  • 2000 - Wins his first of 3 consecutive NBA titles, dubbing the term "3-Peat" and becoming the youngest player to ever do so.
  • 2002 - Leads Western Conference to victory and wins All-Star MVP.
  • 2003 - His wife, Vanessa, gives birth to the first of their 4 children.
  • 2003 - Kobe is charged with sexual assault involving a hotel employee but these charges were dropped a year later.
  • 2008 - Is voted League MVP followed by an Olympic Gold Medal win.
  • 2009 - Wins another NBA Finals, achieving his first Finals MVP and repeats this feat the following year.
  • 2012 - Wins 2nd Olympic Gold Medal.
  • 2012 - Passes Michael Jordan on all-time scoring list as #3.
  • 2016 - Plays in final career game and notches 60 points in a win to go out strong.
  • 2017 - Lakers retire both his #8 and #24 jerseys.
  • 2018 - Wins an Oscar for best short animated film -- "Dear Basketball" based on a poem he authored.
  • 2020 - Kobe, along with his 13 year old daughter, Gianna, are among 9 people killed in a helicopter crash in Calabassas, California.
